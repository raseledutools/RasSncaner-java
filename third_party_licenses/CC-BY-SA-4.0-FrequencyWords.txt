Included Dictionary Files
-----------------------------------------------------------

The following language dictionaries are included:

- ara.txt.gz    (Arabic)
- ces.txt.gz    (Czech)
- chi_sim.txt.gz (Chinese Simplified)
- chi_tra.txt.gz (Chinese Traditional)
- dan.txt.gz    (Danish)
- deu.txt.gz    (German)
- eng.txt.gz    (English)
- fas.txt.gz    (Persian)
- fra.txt.gz    (French)
- hun.txt.gz    (Hungarian)
- ita.txt.gz    (Italian)
- nld.txt.gz    (Dutch)
- nor.txt.gz    (Norwegian)
- pol.txt.gz    (Polish)
- por.txt.gz    (Portuguese)
- ron.txt.gz    (Romanian)
- rus.txt.gz    (Russian)
- slk.txt.gz    (Slovak)
- spa.txt.gz    (Spanish)
- swe.txt.gz    (Swedish)
- tha.txt.gz    (Thai)
- tur.txt.gz    (Turkish)

Each file contains the 50,000 most frequent words for the respective language.
