MakeACopy 4.1.0
* Improved OCR architecture with separate Standard and Paddle implementations.
* Enhanced Paddle OCR processing and safer crop validation.
* Improved OCR review reading order, including RTL-aware word ordering and rendering.
* Improved searchable PDF export with a fallback text layer when word boxes are unavailable.
* Refined RTL handling so TXT export keeps logical text while PDF export applies PDF-specific reordering.
* Paddle builds now include the correct edition assets alongside Paddle assets.
* Note: Paddle builds are currently available on GitHub only.
